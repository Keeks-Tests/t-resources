Fixation Point Emphasis

Bionic Style

This method bolds the first few letters of every word, creating artificial fixation points. Your brain will automatically complete the rest of the word, allowing your eyes to glide across the text faster.

Text Chunking

Peripheral Vision

Chunking trains you to read groups of words at a single glance rather than reading word-by-word. Try to focus your eyes on the center of each grey block.

Rapid Serial Visual Presentation (RSVP)

Subvocalization Reduction

RSVP flashes words one at a time exactly where your eyes are focused. This eliminates eye movement completely and helps silence the "inner voice" (subvocalization) that slows you down.
